Sample Question 1

Topic: Thevenin Equivalent

  • Question: Find the Thevenin equivalent across the load terminals.
  • Step 1: Remove the load resistor.
  • Step 2: Calculate open-circuit voltage.
  • Step 3: Find equivalent resistance.
  • Final Answer: Write the Thevenin voltage and resistance clearly.

Topic: Convolution

  • Question: Compute the convolution of two given signals.
  • Step 1: Write both signals properly.
  • Step 2: Find overlap intervals.
  • Step 3: Integrate over the correct limits.
  • Final Answer: Present the result neatly.

Topic: Transformer Efficiency

  • Step 1: Determine output power.

  • Step 3: Use the efficiency formula.
  • Final Answer: Express efficiency in percentage.
